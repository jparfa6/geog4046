New Wonders of the World

The new Wonders of the World were selected in 2007. The selection process involved voting on 200 monuments that exist throughout the world.There are traditionally 7 Wonders of the World. However, an 8th was selected as an "Honorary Wonder". The Honorary Wonder is the only remaining member of the Ancient Wonders of the World.

This map shows the locations of the new Wonders of the World. The shapes of the wonders are color coded to represent the time period they were constructed; purple shapes indicate they were constructed before A.D. 1, pink shapes were constructed between A.D. 1 and A.D. 1000, blue shapes were constructed between the years 1001 and 1899, and green shapes were constructed post 1900.
